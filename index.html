Master Thesis

The theory of rough paths is an important tool in solving Stochastic Differential Equations. We focuse on an approach to controlled ODEs with a driving path of Hölder regularity greater than 1/3, based on the Sewing Bound and appropriate a priori estimates. We take the first steps towards the extension of the theory to paths with arbitrarily low Hölder regularity.
