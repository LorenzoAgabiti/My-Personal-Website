I have done my bachelor at Politecnico di Torino and then I moved at Epfl for my master in applied Mathematics with a minor in financial engineering. Master Thesis

The theory of rough paths is an important tool in solving Stochastic Differential Equations. This point of view gives an elegant framework to treat SDEs as controlled ordinary differential equations with an irregular driving path. In the master thesis we focused on an approach to controlled ODEs with a driving path of H√∂lder regularity greater than 1/3, based on the Sewing Bound and appropriate a priori estimates. We take the first steps towards the extension of the theory to paths with arbitrarily low H√∂lder regularity.
